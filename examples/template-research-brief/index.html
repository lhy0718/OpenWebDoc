Research brief

Browser-native evidence brief

Use this template for a compact research memo that combines a claim, evidence table, decision notes, and follow-up questions in one portable HTMLX package.

Audience: product and research leads Length: 4 to 6 sections Status: draft-ready

Primary takeaway

Put the conclusion first. The rest of the brief should explain what changed, why it matters, how strong the evidence is, and what decision the reader can make now.

Open question

Frame the uncertainty clearly enough that another reviewer or external agent can refine the brief without inventing private context.
