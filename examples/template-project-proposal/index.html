Project proposal

Portable document workflow pilot

A proposal format for explaining why a team should adopt HTMLX packages, what will be tested, what is out of scope, and how success will be measured.

NeedDocuments should open in browsers without losing structure.
ApproachUse `.htmlx` packages with local assets and metadata.
ValidationRun CLI checks before sharing or archiving.
OutcomeReadable, editable, and agent-friendly document bundles.

Pilot plan

Phase Deliverable Review question
Prepare Template gallery and validated examples Can first-time users understand the format?
